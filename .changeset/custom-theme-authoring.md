Document custom theme authoring. Consumers can define their own palette (colors, rounding, fonts, …) by overriding the documented token contract under a `data-theme` selector — same mechanism as the built-in `bureau` theme. Adds `docs/authoring-themes.md` (token contract + guide) and a copy-paste `tokens/theme-template.css` starting point.
